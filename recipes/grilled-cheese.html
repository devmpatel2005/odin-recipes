Grilled Cheese

◀Go Back

Description

A grilled cheese is a hot sandwich made with cheese between slices of bread. It's usually cooked on the stove until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Put butter on both pieces of the bread
  2. Toast both pieces of bread on the stove
  3. Take off the bread and put cheese inbetween the 2 pieces
  4. Put the sandwich back on the stove until the cheese melts
  5. Take off the grilled cheese and cut it
  6. Enjoy!
